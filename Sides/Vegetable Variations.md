# Vegetable Variations

## Ingredients
- Whatever vegetable you want to use.  Suggestions:
  - frozen mixed vegetables 
  - zucchini
  - brocolli
  - carrots
  
## Recipe
1. Cook vegetable.  Steam, saute, roast.
2. Add a variation below.

## Variations

### Spicy Honey
- cayenne
- honey
- lemon juice

### Cheese
- parmesean
- cheddar

### Butter
- butter

### Indian style
- cumin
- curry

### Fresh Herbs
- parsley
- tarragon
- any other herbs from garden
