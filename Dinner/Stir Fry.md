# Basic Stir Fry
## Ingredients
- rice
- onions
- vegetables
- eggs

## Recipe
1. Cook rice.
2. Fry onions, then vegetables, then egg.
3. Prepare a sauce (ingredients below - heat all ingredients in separate pot) and combine with stir fry.
